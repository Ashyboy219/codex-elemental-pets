## Reddit / OpenAI Developer Forum

Title: I made two fully animated elemental pets for Codex: Frostbyte and Bolt

Body:

I wanted to extend the elemental pet family with two personalities that felt visually distinct but still native to the same world.

- Frostbyte is a balanced, crystalline ice pet for careful, precise work.
- Bolt is an asymmetric lightning pet for fast experiments and brave first passes.

These are complete v2 pet atlases, not single character images: 11 rows, 73 populated frames each, nine semantic animation states, 16 look directions, transparency cleanup, deterministic validation, continuity review, and independent direction QA.

The public repository includes one-command installation, the spritesheets, manifests, previews, social assets, and the media build script:
